At Cruzian Multi-Purpose Cooperative, we strive to empower local farmers and agrarian reform beneficiaries through innovative solutions and diverse services. Established in 1990 with just ₱1,000 and 36 members, we have grown into a trusted organization with over 90 farmer-members and ₱43 million in assets. Our services include production loans, agricultural trading, rice milling, and more. We are committed to fostering sustainable growth for our members and communities, contributing to national food security, and building a brighter future for Filipino farmers.

How It Works

Register

Register as an Owner or Renter: Sign up as an Owner to list machinery or as a Renter to browse and reserve machines.

Browse and Reserve

Renters can explore machinery listings, select rental dates, and send reservation requests for approval.

Reservation Confirmation

After approval, the machine is booked, and the Owner coordinates delivery with an operator to assist during the rental.

Review the Machinery

After the rental period, Renters can review the machinery they used, helping maintain quality standards and providing feedback.
